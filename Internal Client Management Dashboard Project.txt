Internal Client Management Dashboard Project
Internal Client Management Dashboard Project 
1. Project Objective
The objective of this project is to build a centralized internal dashboard for managing all client-related operations within the agency.
The system will help streamline:
* Client management
* Team accountability
* Communication tracking
* Task management
* Minutes of Meeting (MOM)
* Statement of Work (SOW) tracking
* Client reporting
* Escalation handling
* Client health monitoring
* Management visibility
The platform will act as a single source of truth for all client operations and internal coordination.
________________


2. Business Goals
The proposed dashboard aims to solve the following operational challenges:
1. Scattered client information across tools and spreadsheets
2. Lack of centralized communication history
3. Missed deadlines and follow-ups
4. Poor visibility into task ownership
5. Difficulty tracking deliverables against SOW
6. Delayed reporting and approvals
7. Escalation handling gaps
8. Limited visibility into client risk or health
9. Management dependency on manual updates
10. Lack of structured client lifecycle tracking
________________


3. Proposed System Overview
The dashboard will function as an internal CRM and operations platform specifically designed for digital marketing agency workflows.
The system will provide:
Centralized Client Management
A complete client repository with all associated information in one place.
Team Collaboration
Clear task ownership, communication tracking, and accountability.
Performance Monitoring
Visibility into deadlines, escalation risks, and client satisfaction.
Operational Transparency
Management-level visibility into client delivery and team execution.
Scalable Internal Operations
A foundation that can later support automation and AI-assisted workflows.
________________


4. User Roles & Access Management
The system will include role-based access control to ensure security and structured ownership.
1. Admin
Access Level: Full System Access
Responsibilities:
* User management
* Role permissions
* Client creation and assignment
* SOW approval
* Escalation oversight
* Reports access
* Dashboard analytics
* System configuration

2. Account Manager
Access Level: Assigned Clients
Responsibilities:
* Manage assigned clients
* Maintain communication logs
* Create and assign tasks
* Update SOW progress
* Conduct meetings and MOM
* Upload reports
* Raise escalations
* Monitor client health
________________


3. Team Member
Access Level: Task-Based Access
Responsibilities:
* View assigned tasks
* Update progress
* Submit work updates
* Add implementation notes
* Access assigned client details
________________


4. Management
Access Level: Monitoring & Reporting
Responsibilities:
* View all clients
* Monitor team performance

* Track client health

* Monitor delivery status
